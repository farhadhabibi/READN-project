Where we focus

Programs and Services

Education

Providing ESL (English as a Second Language) classes and literacy programs to enhance communication skills and educational opportunities.

Citizenship Training

Guiding refugees through the citizenship process with training, legal assistance, and resources.

Employment Services

Offering job training, resume building, and interview preparation to improve employability and secure meaningful employment.

Driving Opportunity

Offering driving lessons and educational resources to help refugees obtain driver's licenses and gain independence.

Cultural Adjustment

Facilitating cultural Adjustment programs, community events, and mentorship to help refugees adapt and integrate into their new surroundings.

Health Support

Providing access to mental health services, counseling, and support groups to promote emotional well-being.

READN

Refugee Empowerment and Development Network (REDN) offers essential support for refugees, including literacy, language skills, driving education, mental health care, job readiness, citizenship training, and cultural adjustment. We empower refugees with the tools to thrive in their new community and build a resilient future.
